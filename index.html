The Group

We build brands worth keeping.

FoundryState is a founder-operated holding company on California's central coast. We design, launch, and run businesses across three lines — hospitality, apparel, and media — and we hold them for the long term. The same team that opens a restaurant films it, dresses its people, and builds the brand around it. One operator, one standard, compounding across every venture.

The Approach
01

Build to hold.

We start companies we intend to own for decades, not flip. Patience is the strategy.
